Bohemian Door Curtain with Celestial Designs

Enhance your home's charm with this enchanting door curtain, designed to bring a touch of magic and serenity to any room. The beautiful sun, moon, and star patterns create a soft yet elegant ambiance, making it the perfect choice for bedrooms, living rooms, or bathrooms.

Featuring a 3D printed construction, this curtain is crafted with rich, vibrant colors that stand out and add a whimsical vibe to your interior. The grommet top ensures effortless installation on most curtain rods, while the 100% polyester fabric offers durability and a soothing dim effect by blocking up to 50% of sunlight. It's a stylish solution for both privacy and decor, blending functionality with aesthetic appeal.

  • Celestial Motifs: Each panel is adorned with intricate sun, moon, and star patterns that create a magical cosmic effect.
