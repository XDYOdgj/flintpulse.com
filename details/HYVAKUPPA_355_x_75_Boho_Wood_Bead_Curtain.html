HYVAKUPPA Boho Wood Bead Curtain

Elevate your space with this stunning handmade boho wood bead curtain. Combining beauty with practicality, it’s the perfect blend of style and functionality for any home or event.

Aesthetic & Functional Design

The HYVAKUPPA Boho Wood Bead Curtain boasts a mesmerizing 35.5" x 75" size with 40 intricately crafted wooden beads. The dark brown finish adds a warm, earthy tone that effortlessly complements your interior design. Whether you're looking for a room divider, doorway decoration, or a statement piece, this curtain offers a perfect balance of elegance and usability.
