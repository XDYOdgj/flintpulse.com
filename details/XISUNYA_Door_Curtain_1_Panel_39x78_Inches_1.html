Elegant and Functional Door Curtain

Enhance your home's style and privacy with the XISUNYA Door Curtain. Perfect for both day and night, it helps protect your furniture and decor from harmful UV rays.

Crafted from durable 100% polyester, this curtain is built to last and features a large 1.6-inch grommet top for easy and smooth sliding over any standard curtain rod.
