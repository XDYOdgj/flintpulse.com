AiFish Door Curtain Panel - Stylish & Functional Light Blocking Solution

Enhance your space with the AiFish Door Curtain Panel, the perfect blend of style and functionality for any room. Designed to block up to 90% of light, this curtain panel offers superior privacy and a calm, cozy atmosphere, making it ideal for bedrooms, living rooms, or kids rooms.

Constructed from durable 100% polyester, the triple-weave fabric ensures exceptional light control and thermal insulation. Whether it's keeping your space cooler in the summer or warmer in the winter, these curtains provide a comfortable and elegant solution for every season.
