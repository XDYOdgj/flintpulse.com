StangH Velvet Blackout Curtain Panels

Transform your space with StangH Velvet Blackout Curtain Panels, the perfect blend of style and functionality. These ultra-soft 300 GSM velvet curtains add a touch of elegance while effectively blocking sunlight and UV rays to create a calmer, more inviting atmosphere in any room.

Premium Quality & Superior Performance

With up to 85% blackout efficiency, these curtains are ideal for bedrooms, living rooms, and offices. They offer excellent privacy and help reduce glare, allowing you to enjoy a restful environment without the intrusion of harsh sunlight.

Versatile Design for Every Space

Whether you need a room divider, window treatment, or a way to enhance privacy, these panels are adaptable. They look great in modern and traditional settings, making them a stylish and practical choice for any home or office.

Comfort Meets Practicality

Featuring thick and dense velvet fabric, these curtains provide warmth and noise reduction, helping to keep your space more comfortable. They also offer insulation that contributes to better temperature regulation and a peaceful living environment.

Easy to Install and Maintain

Designed for convenience, these panels come in rod pocket and back loop tab styles, ensuring a simple and secure setup. Plus, they are both machine washable and steam iron-friendly, making them easy to care for in your daily routine.

Why Choose StangH?

StangH offers a complete solution with their Velvet Blackout Curtain Panels. Combining the soft texture and luxurious feel of velvet with the practical benefits of blackout efficiency, these curtains deliver both comfort and function for modern living spaces.
