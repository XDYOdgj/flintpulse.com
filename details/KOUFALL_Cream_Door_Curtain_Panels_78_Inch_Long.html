KOUFALL Cream Door Curtain Panels

Add a touch of elegance and style to your home with these versatile door curtain panels. Designed for both beauty and function, they are perfect for creating a soft, private atmosphere in any room.

Premium Fabric for Softness and Durability

These door curtains are made from a 20% linen and 80% polyester blend, offering the softness of linen with the strength and resilience of polyester. The fabric is light and airy, making it ideal for adding a gentle, flowing effect to your decor.

Light Diffusing Sheer Design

The subtle sheerness of the fabric allows natural light to filter through, creating a warm and inviting ambiance while maintaining a level of privacy. This makes them especially great for spaces like bathrooms, bedrooms, and entryways.

Easy Installation with Tab Top Design

With a tab top design, these curtains can be easily installed on any doorway rod up to 1.5 inches in diameter. They come as a pair with five loops on each panel, ensuring they hang perfectly and securely.

Perfect for Any Home Style

Whether you're into rustic charm, coastal vibes, modern farmhouse aesthetics, or shabby chic decor, these curtain panels seamlessly blend into your space. Their crisper tones add a refined look to any interior.

Low Maintenance for Everyday Use

Thanks to their machine washable and low maintenance property, these door curtains are easy to care for. They stay fresh and beautiful with regular use, making them a practical and stylish choice for your home.

Elevate Your Space with Privacy and Style

Experience the perfect balance of privacy and aesthetic appeal. These door curtain panels offer a refined and soft look while keeping your space comfortable and private. Ideal for those who value style and simplicity in their home decor.
