Elevate Your Space with MIULEE Sheer Door Curtains

Designed for both style and practicality, these MIULEE sheer door curtains offer a seamless blend of elegance and functionality. Perfect for narrow windows, they bring soft light filtration and a romantic touch to any room, whether it's your bathroom, living room, or bedroom.

Key Features

  • Ready-made design with 2 panels and 2 adjustable adhesive tiebacks
  • Sheer curtain fabric made of high-quality polyester for soft and durable use
  • Dual rod pockets (1" each) for secure and adjustable installation
  • Privacy-friendly with proper pleating for subtle light obstruction
  • Elegant, minimalist look that complements both modern and traditional decor

Why Choose These Curtains?

These sheer door curtains are an ideal choice for anyone looking to enhance the aesthetic of their narrow windows. The sheer and semi-transparent fabric allows natural light to pass through, creating a bright and inviting atmosphere. At the same time, they offer a subtle sense of privacy when pleated, making them perfect for bathrooms or private areas.

With their easy-to-install design and adjustable tiebacks, you can customize the appearance to match your personal style. The beige color adds a warm touch to any room, blending effortlessly with your existing decor. Whether you're aiming for a modern, minimalist vibe or something more traditional, these curtains deliver both beauty and comfort.
